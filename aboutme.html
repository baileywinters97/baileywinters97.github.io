About Me

I have loved Art for as long as I can remember. My discovered my love for Art when I got a coloring book and the pack of crayons with the crayon sharpener (THE BEST!!!) My entire life I have wanted to use my Art to help people, which is why I chose Graphic Design as my profession. I am extremely passionate about what I do and I want to continue doing what I love for the people in my life.
